O U R   S T O R Y

We love sheep and we love farming!

We are from a long generations of farmers, working with sheep for over a centure, and passionated per our land. But we are not innocent, we know that the way we produce wool and farm can change the future of farming. That's why 15 years ago we decide to change, for the best. And learn how to farm with enviromental responsability. We have found the Responsible Wool Standard (RWS), which ensures that farms use best practices to protect the land, and treat the animal decently.

Water for your animals.

We don't waste water! We use water from the rain, we don't dump waterwate into the river or soil and we don't use toxic substances to colour our wool. Our farms sick with EU environmental regulations and as our wool is organic and natural, its don't require any chemicals to be produced.

  • Keep it personal

    In order to mantain our standards of caring, we only work with local suppliers and keep a cooperative to help all our neighbours that need some job.

    We also teach our kids the importance of being sustanable and how things work around here, so they can continue our work in the future. Responsability is a tough lesson that we learn at home.

    If you work with kids and want to teach them about sustainable farming, give us a call and we can arrange a visit for your group. Let's spread knowlege.
