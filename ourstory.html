O U R   S T O R Y

We are from a long generation of farmers, working with sheep for over a century. We are passionate about our land. But we are not innocent, we know that the way we produce wool and they way we farm can change the future of farming.

That is why 15 years ago we decided to change, for the best. By learning how to farm with environmental responsibility. We have founded the Responsible Wool Standard (RWS), which ensures that farms use the best practices to protect the land, and that their animals recieve the best care.

We don't waste water! We use rainwater and we do not dump waterwaste into rivers or soil. We do not use toxic substances to colour our wool. Our farms follow EU environmental regulations and because our wool is organic and natural, it does not require any chemicals to be produced.

  • In order to maintain our standards of caring, we only work with local suppliers and keep a cooperative to help our local economy flourish.

    We also teach our children the importance of being sustainable and how things work around here, so they can continue our work in the future.

    Responsibility is a tough lesson that we learn at home.

    If you work with children and want to teach them about sustainable farming, give us a call and we can arrange a visit for your group.

    Let's spread knowledge.
