  • The impact of clothing on the environment is becoming more noticeable every day. Currently, the fashion industry is the second largest polluter in the world. Just behind the oil industry. And of course the environmental damage is increasing as the industry grows.

    In most of the countries in which garments are produced, untreated toxic wastewaters from textiles factories are dumped directly into the rivers. For these reasons, we use only organic fibres and natural fibres require no chemicals during production.

    Wool’s natural properties not only mean that products perform well during extended use, but items are also easy to look after; reducing their impact on the environment and extending their useful lifespan.

  • Our sheep thrive in the beautiful (and wet!) Irish climate. They were made for these conditions. However, in order to protect our sheep from stress and to help ensure that they have the best life possible, our farmers provide adequate shade and shelter.

    Raised on free-range farms, our sheep forage as they please. Sheep which recieve proper nutrition are better able to cope with the often tough weather Ireland is known for.

    Our sheep always have access to clean water and proper nutrition and our farmers regularly monitor their flock, prevent disease and illness, and rapidly diagnose any health issues—essential in minimising stress and discomfort.

    Our farmers pride themselves on their knowlegdge which has been passed down over several generations of sheep farmers.

  • The fibres we process here at Woollen Mill & Co. are uniform in diameter, which makes the wool strong. This means that we provide consistent, high quality, high performing fibre year on year, season after season.



    These natural advantages are passed onto you, through our products, that will keep you cosy and comfortable and maintaining their durability with whatever life passes your way.
