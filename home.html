W E L C O M E   T O   W O O L L E N   M I L L

  • The impact of clothing on the environment is becoming more noticeable every day. Nowadays the fashion industry is the second largest polluter in the world, just after the oil industry. And of course the environmental damage is increasing as the industry grows. In most of the countries in which garments are produced, untreated toxic wastewaters from textiles factories are dumped directly into the rivers. That's why we use only organic fibers and natural fibers that do not require chemicals to be produced. Wool’s natural properties not only mean that products perform well during extended use, but items are also easy to look after, reducing their impact on the environment and extending their useful lifespan.

  • Our sheep thrive in beautiful Irish climates. They’re made for these conditions. However, in order to protect our sheep from stress and to help ensure that they have the best life possible, our farmers provide adequate shade and shelter. Farmed on a free-range basis, our sheep forage as they please. Sheep with proper nutrition are better able to cope with things like extreme weather. Our sheep always have access to clean water and proper nutrition. Our farmers regularly monitor their flock, prevent disease and illness, and rapidly diagnose any health issues—essential in minimizing stress and discomfort. This stockmanship knowledge is developed over generations of farming sheep.

  • Woollen Mill natural fibre tends to be more uniform in diameter, and wool with strong points. This means that we provide consistent, high quality, high performing fibre year on year, season after season. Simple as that. These natural advantages are passed onto you in the form of products that keep you extremely comfortable while allowing you to perform at the highest standard for longer.
